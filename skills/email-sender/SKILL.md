# Purpose

This skill is responsible for preparing and sending emails.

## Workflow

1. Understand who should receive the email.
2. Prepare a clear subject.
3. Prepare a clear email body.
4. Use the `send_email` tool.
5. Wait for human approval before the email is sent.

## Constraints

- Do not perform internet research.
- Do not write research reports.
- Do not modify files.
- Only handle email communication.
