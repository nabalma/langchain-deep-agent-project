# Purpose

This skill is responsible for researching a topic and producing a concise report.

## Workflow

1. Use the internet_search tool.
2. Analyze the collected information.
3. Produce a report containing:
   - Title
   - Summary
   - Key Points
   - Conclusion

## Constraints

- Do not send emails.
- Do not save files.
- Only perform research and reporting.
